Sweet and Sour Sauce

Description

Sweet and sour sauce is a condiment, commonly used in Asian cuisines, that combines (you guessed it!) sweet and sour flavors. It can be used as a dipping sauce, a glaze, or as an ingredient in marinades and so much more. Sweet and sour sauce is commonly associated with sweet and sour chicken, a staple on Chinese-American menus.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup water
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place sugar, water, vinegar, soy sauce, cornstarch, and ketchup in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Stir continuously until mixture has thickened.
  3. Enjoy!
